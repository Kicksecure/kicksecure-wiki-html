Trademark Policy[edit]

Version: 1.0
Published: 22 February 2019

ENCRYPTED SUPPORT LP owns a number of trademarks in both word and logo form including brands, slogans, styles. This policy encompasses all marks, in word and logo form, collectively referred to as "Kicksecure ™ trademarks". You can see a non-exhaustive list of Kicksecure ™ trademarks, including both registered and unregistered (but otherwise legally recognized) trademarks at in chapter Trademarks.

The objective of this trademark policy is:

  1. to encourage widespread use and adoption of the Kicksecure ™ trademarks,
  2. to clarify proper usage of Kicksecure ™ trademarks by third parties,
  3. to prevent misuse of Kicksecure ™ trademarks that can confuse or mislead users with respect to Kicksecure ™ or its affiliates.

Please note that it is not the goal of this policy to limit commercial activity around Kicksecure ™. We encourage businesses to work on Kicksecure ™ while being compliant with this policy.

Following are the guidelines for the proper use of Kicksecure ™ trademarks by publishers and other third parties. Any use of or reference to Kicksecure ™ trademarks that is inconsistent with these guidelines, or other unauthorized use of or reference to Kicksecure ™ trademarks, or use of marks that are confusingly similar to Kicksecure ™ trademarks, is prohibited and may violate Kicksecure ™ trademark rights.

Any use of Kicksecure ™ trademarks in a misleading and false manner or in a manner that disparages Kicksecure ™, such as untruthful advertising, is always prohibited.

When You Can Use the Kicksecure ™ Trademarks Without Asking Permission[edit]

  1. You can use Kicksecure ™ trademarks to make true factual statements about Kicksecure ™ or communicate compatibility with your product truthfully.
  2. Your intended use qualifies as "nominative fair use" of the Kicksecure ™ trademarks, i.e., merely identifying that you are talking about Kicksecure ™ in a text, without suggesting sponsorship or endorsement.
  3. You can use Kicksecure ™ trademarks to describe or advertise your services or products relating to Kicksecure ™ in a way that is not misleading.
  4. You can use Kicksecure ™ trademarks to describe Kicksecure ™ in articles, titles or blog posts.
  5. You can make t-shirts, desktop wallpapers, caps, or other merchandise with Kicksecure ™ trademarks for both, commercial and non-commercial usage.
  6. You can also make merchandise with Kicksecure ™ trademarks for both, commercial and non-commercial usage. In case of commercial usage, we recommend that you truthfully advertise to customers which part of the selling price, if any, will be paid to the Kicksecure ™ project. See our Payments page for more information on how to donate to the Kicksecure ™ project.

When You Can NEVER Use the Kicksecure ™ Trademarks Without Asking Permission[edit]

  1. You cannot use Kicksecure ™ trademarks in any way that suggests an affiliation with or endorsement by the Kicksecure ™ project or community, if the same is not true.
  2. You cannot use Kicksecure ™ trademarks in a company or organization name or as the name of a product or service.
  3. You cannot use a name that is confusingly similar to Kicksecure ™ trademarks.
  4. You cannot use Kicksecure ™ trademarks in a domain name, with or without commercial intent.

How to Use the Kicksecure ™ Trademarks[edit]

1. Use the Kicksecure ™ trademarks in a manner that makes it clear that your project is related to the Kicksecure ™ project, but that it is not part of Kicksecure ™, produced by the Kicksecure ™ project, or endorsed by the Kicksecure ™ project.

2. Acknowledge ENCRYPTED SUPPORT LP's ownership of the Kicksecure ™ trademark prominently.

Example:

[TRADEMARK] is a trademark owned by ENCRYPTED SUPPORT LP.

3. Include a disclaimer of sponsorship, affiliation, or endorsement by Kicksecure ™ on your website and on all related printed materials.

Example:

X PROJECT is not affiliated with Kicksecure ™. Kicksecure ™ is a trademark owned by ENCRYPTED SUPPORT LP.

4. Distinguish the Kicksecure ™ trademarks from the surrounding words by italicizing, bolding or underlining it.

5. Use the Kicksecure ™ trademarks in their exact form, neither abbreviated or hyphenated, nor combined with any other word or words.

6. Do not create acronyms using the Kicksecure ™ trademarks.

Permission To Use[edit]

When in doubt about the use of Kicksecure ™ trademarks, or to request permission for uses not allowed by this policy, please send an email to adrelanos@kicksecure.com with subject "Trademark Use Request"; be sure to include the following information in the body of your message:

  • Name of the User
  • Name of the organization/project
  • Purpose of Use (commercial/non-commercial)
  • Nature of Use

Newer Versions of this Policy[edit]

This policy may be revised from time to time and updated versions shall be available at ./Trademark_Policy.

Guidelines for Using Logos[edit]

  • Any scaling must retain the original proportions of the logo.
  • Do not use the Kicksecure ™ logos as part of your company logo or product logo or branding itself. They can be used as part of a page describing your products or services.
  • You need not ask us for permission to use logos on your own website solely as a hyperlink to the Kicksecure ™ project website.

For any queries with respect to these guidelines, please send an email to adrelanos@kicksecure.com.
